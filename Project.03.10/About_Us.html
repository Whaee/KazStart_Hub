Our Goals

To collect business and financial information about Kazakh startups. This resource helps investors, entrepreneurs,
and analysts access up-to-date data on local ventures, facilitating effective interaction and support for innovative projects in Kazakhstan.

Our mission

At KazStartHub, our mission is to empower Kazakhstan's entrepreneurial ecosystem by providing comprehensive, up-to-date
business and financial information about local startups. We aim to facilitate effective interaction and support for
innovative projects, fostering a collaborative environment where investors, entrepreneurs, and analysts can thrive.v
